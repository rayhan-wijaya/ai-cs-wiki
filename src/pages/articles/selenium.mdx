_Selenium_, nicknamed "Selene", is an android of _Cybrix_ descent and one of
the main characters during _On A King's Pedestal_, _Set Foot Blind_, and
_Concentrated Solitude_. She partners with _Arsenic_ throughout the novels she
is involved in except for _Concentrated Solitude_, where she is on her own.
